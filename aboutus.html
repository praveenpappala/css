About Us

Pappala Praveen is Director and Founder of our Organisation. He has over 10 years experience in India and UK. He worked with Raam group Organisation which is started as a normal car show and now expanded as a Multi-national car retailer. He has been inspired and motivated on a dialy basis to help brands grow.

Mercedes-Benz is one of our partner, commonly referred to as Mercedes, is both a German automotive brand and, from late 2019 onwards, a subsidiary – as Mercedes-Benz AG – of Daimler AG. Mercedes-Benz is known for producing luxury vehicles and commercial vehicles.

Tata Motors Limited is also one of our partners, is an Indian multinational automotive manufacturing company headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. It is a part of Tata Group, an Indian conglomerate. Its products include passenger cars, trucks, vans, coaches, buses, sports cars, construction equipment and military vehicles
